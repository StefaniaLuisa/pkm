When you're recommending a contract increase, especially in the non-profit space where budget justification is critical, it’s key to frame the ask around **strategic value and impact**, not just hours. We’re not just asking for more — we’re offering a roadmap to significantly increase their digital effectiveness.

Here’s a cleaned-up and strategically positioned draft you can use as a foundation for your proposal/conversation:

---

### Proposed Monthly Hour Increase: From X to 70 Hours

As we look ahead to renewing our engagement, we believe there’s a strong case for expanding our monthly hours to **70 hours/month**. This would allow us to move from maintenance mode into **strategic growth and optimization** — activating the priorities we’ve already identified together and tackling some high-impact opportunities that we’ve had to delay due to budget limitations.

Here’s how we propose to use the expanded hours to deliver measurable value:

---

### 1. **Analytics Dashboard Review + Optimization (GA4)**

- **Status:** Audit and initial dashboard build are complete.

- **Opportunity:** We’ve identified areas for refinement and new data visualization, but paused for budget reasons.

- **Value:** A clearer picture of user behavior, campaign performance, and donation form engagement — better decision-making, less guessing.

---

### 2. **Custom GA4 Events (via Google Tag Manager)**

- **Status:** Limited events currently being tracked.

- **Opportunity:** Implement tracking on key interactions (button clicks, resource downloads, outbound links, etc.) that align with programmatic and fundraising goals.

- **Value:** Fills in critical data gaps; turns the website into a more powerful insight engine.

---

### 3. **Ongoing SEO Optimization**

- **Status:** 1 hour/week is insufficient to make meaningful progress.

- **Recommendation:** Shift to **3 hours every 2 weeks** to enable deeper audits, implementation, and monitoring.

- **Value:** Better organic reach, more mission-aligned traffic, and long-term visibility gains.

---

### 4. **Promo Types Build-Out + A/B Testing**

- **Status:** Only partial implementation of "Promo Types" (homegrown content/promo components).

- **Opportunity:** Finish building out all templates, and test messaging/design variations.

- **Value:** Improved engagement with key messages, higher conversions for actions or donations.

---

### 5. **Subscription Optimization (Website + EveryAction)**

- **Exploration Area:** We'd like to revisit how email subscriptions are being captured and synced across systems.

- **Opportunity:** Review opt-in UX, audience segmentation, and connection to email journeys.

- **Value:** Healthier email list growth, better targeting, and higher engagement.

---

### 6. **Strategic Retrospective + Digital Pain Point Assessment**

- In our next conversation, we’d love to explore:

  - What digital roadblocks did the team face over the past year?

  - Did you feel confident in your digital outreach, landing pages, and promotions?

  - Were there moments where you said “I wish we had ___ to engage our community”?

We want to make sure any increase in investment is going to the places that matter most to your mission. This reflection helps prioritize our roadmap and ensures our hours are used as strategically as possible.
