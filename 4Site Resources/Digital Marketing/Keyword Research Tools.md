# Keyword Research Tools

#strategy #maketingtools #SEO #resources 

Research your top keywords.
Landing content in front of your ideal customer is challenging unless you know what they’re searching for. <u>[97% of the top Pinterest searches](https://business.pinterest.com/audience/?utm_campaign=Marketing%2520Blog%2520-%2520Daily%2520Emails&utm_medium=email&utm_content=286249669&utm_source=hs_email)</u> are unbranded, which means you need to use keywords other than your brand name.
To compete on SEO, research which keywords your audience uses to browse online. First, make a list of keywords used in your business.
Then, use a keyword research tool to see how much search volume those have and look for related ideas.
You can start with free tools like <u>[Keyword Surfer](https://surferseo.com/keyword-surfer-extension/?utm_campaign=Marketing%2520Blog%2520-%2520Daily%2520Emails&utm_medium=email&utm_content=286249669&utm_source=hs_email)</u> or <u>[Google Ads Keyword Planner](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/google-ads-keyword-planner?utm_campaign=Marketing%2520Blog%2520-%2520Daily%2520Emails&utm_medium=email&utm_content=286249669&utm_source=hs_email&hubs_content=blog.hubspot.com/blog/tabid/6307/bid/34042/the-marketer-s-guide-to-pinterest-seo.aspx&hubs_content-cta=Google%20Ads%20Keyword%20Planner)</u>. For more tips, check out HubSpot’s <u>[keyword research for beginners guide](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/how-to-do-keyword-research-ht?utm_campaign=Marketing%2520Blog%2520-%2520Daily%2520Emails&utm_medium=email&utm_content=286249669&utm_source=hs_email&hubs_content=blog.hubspot.com/blog/tabid/6307/bid/34042/the-marketer-s-guide-to-pinterest-seo.aspx&hubs_content-cta=keyword%20research%20for%20beginners%20guide)</u> and <u>[competitor research tutorial](https://blog.hubspot.com/website/10-best-online-tools-spying-competitors-traffic?utm_campaign=Marketing%2520Blog%2520-%2520Daily%2520Emails&utm_medium=email&utm_content=286249669&utm_source=hs_email&hubs_content=blog.hubspot.com/blog/tabid/6307/bid/34042/the-marketer-s-guide-to-pinterest-seo.aspx&hubs_content-cta=competitor%20research%20tutorial)</u>.
