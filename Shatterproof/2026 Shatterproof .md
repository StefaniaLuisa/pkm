  ## Meeting Purpose

  [Plan the website migration from Drupal to WordPress.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=248.0)

  ## Key Takeaways
  - [**Hard Deadline:** A 6-month Drupal hosting contract creates a hard deadline, requiring the new site to launch by the end of July to avoid a costly renewal.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=248.0)
  - [**New Strategy:** The project is a "recreate" (not a traditional migration) to build a new site focused on a key new audience: parents and families.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=275.0)
  - [**Memorials Challenge:** The high-traffic Memorials section requires a separate strategy, with options including a custom build, Engaging Networks, or Donor Drive.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1894.0)
  - [**Immediate Action:** Use the 46 remaining retainer hours for a project kickoff and to map the current site, providing a concrete baseline for the RFP process.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1699.0)

  ## Topics

  ### Project Scope & Timeline
  - [**Timeline:** A 6-month Drupal hosting contract creates a hard deadline.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=248.0)
    - [**Goal:** Launch by end of July to create a buffer before the contract expires at the end of September.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=713.0)
  - [**Scope:** A "recreate" project, not a traditional migration.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=275.0)
    - [**Process:** Manually move only approved content to a new WordPress site.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=275.0)
    - [**Rationale:** Ensures a clean start and avoids carrying over legacy issues.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=275.0)
  - [**Content Migration:**](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=657.0)
    - [**Memorials:** Requires a programmatic migration due to volume.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=657.0)
    - [**Other Content:** Will be migrated manually, supported by 3–4 new hires starting in February.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=657.0)

  ### New Website Strategy & Audiences
  - [**Primary Goal:** Create a holistic resource hub for substance use disorder, moving beyond the current site's narrow focus on Atlas and Memorials.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=480.0)
  - [**Key New Audience:** Parents and families.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=470.0)
    - [**Rationale:** Aligns with CEO Pam's strategic vision.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=470.0)
  - [**Audience Definition:** Defining personas and user journeys is the critical first step.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=348.0)
    - [**Blocker:** This definition is pending a meeting tomorrow with CEO Pam.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1760.0)
    - [**Significance:** This work will inform all marketing efforts, not just the website.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=348.0)

  ### Memorials Strategy
  - [**Challenge:** The high-traffic Memorials section requires a dedicated, scalable solution.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1894.0)
  - [**Options Discussed:**](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1894.0)
    - [**Custom Build:** A custom WordPress solution.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1894.0)
    - [**Engaging Networks:** A peer-to-peer (P2P) module could simplify systems by consolidating memorial and walk fundraising.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=2019.0)
    - [**Donor Drive:** The current platform for walks; exploring if it can also support memorials.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=2071.0)

  ### 4Site's Proposed Process
  - [Stef presented 4Site's standard process for website redesigns.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=299.0)
  - [**Kickoff & Intake:** A deep-dive session to define goals and audiences.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=840.0)
  - [**Sitemap & Structure:**](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=896.0)
    - [**Visual Sitemap:** An interactive map of the current site hierarchy.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=896.0)
    - [**Content Plan:** A written document detailing page goals, functionality, and technical notes.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1116.0)
  - [**Design & Mockups:**](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1133.0)
    - [**"Frankenstein" Page:** A single page mockup containing all reusable content blocks.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1133.0)
    - [**Rationale:** Efficiently defines the design system for the entire site.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1133.0)
  - [**Content Migration Plan:** A spreadsheet to track content status (keep, edit, new, delete) and migration method (manual vs. programmatic).](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1273.0)
  - [**Content Templates:** Structured templates for new pages to guide content creation.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1384.0)
  - [**SEO & Redirects:** SEO is integrated throughout the process (e.g., H1/H2 tags). A redirect strategy will be defined for all old URLs.](https://fathom.video/share/-DC_21SWb5rMUNUusoXzbtGe1sHywMzz?tab=summary&timestamp=1632.0)
