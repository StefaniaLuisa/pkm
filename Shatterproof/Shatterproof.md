## Website Redesign Strategy

We will take a phased approach - updating and optimizing the sections of the site that bring the most impact to the user, increasing engagement from the visitor as well as increase the likelihood of a donation being made.

Remove dead part of the website - this will be chosen based on what pages of the website are not longer being visited and have the lowest engagement and conversion rate. We will use platforms like Google Search Console and Google Analytics 4 to determine these areas of the website.

Again using GA4/ Google Search Console, we will review the pages that are visited the most and review the blocks/content and layout of those pages - we will review the responsive aspects of the page and check for any opportunity to optimize the content and enhance any features/elements that might encourage the visitor to take action/engage/donate.
