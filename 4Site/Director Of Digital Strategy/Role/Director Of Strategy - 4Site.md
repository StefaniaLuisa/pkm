---

## **How You’ll Contribute**

**Crafting solutions on Engaging Networks**

Most of our clients use Engaging Networks (EN) to power their fundraising and advocacy efforts. You’ll be responsible for crafting tailored solutions on this platform that align with each client’s strategic goals. A deep understanding of EN is essential for success in this role.

Clients often come to us when they’re unable to implement complex campaigns on their own. They rely on us to devise thoughtful, often highly technical solutions. You’ll work closely with our developers to design robust approaches that balance the project’s ambitions with the client’s budget.

#### **Providing strategy for retainer clients**

As the lead for several long-term and new retainer clients, you will play a central role in shaping and implementing strategic recommendations. Success in this role requires staying current with industry best practices and proactively offering insights that elevate client efforts and uncover new opportunities for growth.

#### **Collaborating with our Project Managers**

You will work closely with our Project Managers to create detailed project plans, including accurate work descriptions and estimates. Together, you’ll ensure projects are scoped effectively and that clear expectations are set for both our team and the client.

#### **Building rapport with our clients**

Fostering strong client relationships is key to our success. Through open communication, a genuine understanding of their needs, and a clear commitment to their goals, you’ll help ensure clients have a positive and collaborative experience working with us.
