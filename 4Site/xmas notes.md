# Personalized Holiday Card Messages for Stef’s Clients

Below are ten distinct, hand‑written holiday card messages for Stef to send to her clients. Each message balances warmth and professionalism, expressing gratitude for the client’s business and wishing them well for the holiday season and year ahead.  The templates draw on best practices for business holiday cards, such as expressing genuine thanks and looking forward to continued partnership .  These examples can be used as‑is or modified to fit each client’s circumstances.

 1. Thanking a loyal client

    > Happy Holidays! Thank you for trusting us with your business this past year. Working with you continues to be a highlight of what we do, and we look forward to another successful year together . Wishing you and yours a festive season filled with peace and joy.

 2. Celebrating a new partnership

    > It’s been a pleasure beginning our partnership this year. We’re grateful for the opportunity to collaborate and excited about the projects ahead. May your holiday season be bright and your new year full of growth and new opportunities.

 3. Appreciating a long‑term client

    > Another year of partnership—thank you for your loyalty. We appreciate your continued trust and friendship. Wishing you a holiday season filled with warmth and a new year full of continued success.

 4. Sending warm wishes to a high‑impact client

    > Your collaboration has pushed us to be better. Thank you for being an essential part of our success story. May your holidays be meaningful and restful, and your new year filled with inspiration and joy.

 5. Recognizing a small or local business

    > Your support means more than you know. As fellow members of our local community, we’re proud to work alongside your business. Wishing you a holiday season filled with warmth, rest, and cheer—and a prosperous new year.

 6. Acknowledging a partner or referrer

    > Working alongside you has been a highlight of our year. Thank you for your trust and referrals and for being a trusted collaborator. Warmest holiday wishes to you, and here’s to more shared success in the coming year.

 7. Sending a concise yet heartfelt greeting

    > Season’s Greetings! Thank you for choosing us—your business makes all the difference . Wishing you joy, peace, and success during the holidays and beyond.

 8. Offering gratitude and optimism

    > As the year draws to a close, we reflect on how grateful we are for clients like you. Your support and collaboration have meant so much . May your holidays be filled with happiness, and may the new year bring exciting opportunities for us both.

 9. Highlighting shared achievements

    > What a year it’s been! Together we have achieved great things, and your commitment has been instrumental in our success . Thank you, and warm wishes for a joyous holiday season and a bright new year ahead.

10. Combining thanks with hope for the future

> Thank you for being a valued client and partner. Your trust and support have helped us grow, and we can’t wait to see what we accomplish together next year. Wishing you and your loved ones a happy holiday and a prosperous start to the new year.

Each of these messages can be personalized further by including the client’s name, mentioning a specific project or success from 2025, or adding a brief note tailored to the industry or relationship.  Keeping the tone sincere and appreciative helps strengthen business relationships while maintaining a professional style.
