## ~🔴Misc. Notes~

### Stupski - Launch Reshare:

Congratulations to Glen Galaich, Claire Callahan, the entire team at the Stupski Foundation, and the many collaborators who contributed to successfully launching, Control: Why Big Giving Falls Short.

On behalf of 4Site Studios, I want to express how proud we are to have played a role in supporting this important project by designing and developing the book’s website and landing page. It is truly an honor to be part of something so needed at this time! 

For those who are unfamiliar, Control: Why Big Giving Falls Short offers a takes a look at the U.S. philanthropic system, highlighting how a small group of wealthy donors maintain control over nearly $2 trillion in charitable funds (sound familiar? It’s pervasive ya’ll). Not surprisingly, these funds are often tightly controlled and are frequently restricted rather than reaching the communities that need them most. 

In the book, Glen shares his personal journey in recognizing that the traditional system is not structured to provide readily available resources to those in greatest need, and how he now advocates for a more community-centered approach to giving (which is super interesting to learn about).

This is the kind of work that inspires and drives meaningful change in not only this sector but the world! Read it, share it!
