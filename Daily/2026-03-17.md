## ~🔴Misc. Notes~

### Stupski - Launch Reshare:

Congratulations to Glen Galaich, Claire Callahan, and the entire team at Stupski Foundation—along with the many collaborators who helped bring this launch to life.

At 4Site Studios, we’re incredibly proud to played a role in supporting this work, designing and creating the book's website/landing page. It’s an honor to be part of something that is not only timely, but deeply necessary.

Control: Why Big Giving Falls Short takes a clear and honest look at how the U.S. philanthropic system enables a small group of wealthy donors to maintain control over nearly $2 trillion in charitable funds. Glen challenges this “mindset of control,” highlighting how it can slow down or restrict the flow of resources to the communities that need them most.

What makes this especially compelling is the personal evolution behind it. Glen shares his own shift—from supporting traditional donor control to advocating for a more community-centered approach to giving. That perspective brings both credibility and urgency to the conversation.

This is the kind of work that pushes the sector forward—thoughtful, reflective, and grounded in real change.

If you’re thinking about the future of philanthropy, this is absolutely worth a read.
