# 2025

## Top Tier SOW Updates

*Monday, December 29*

### Design

Design will follow established PaintCare branding and style guidelines to ensure consistency with the broader site experience.

To streamline review, reduce redundant design effort, and establish a strong, scalable foundation, 4Site will center the design process around one representative Upper Tier page rather than creating multiple parallel mockups of similar layouts.

Design will begin with the Retailers page, which was intentionally selected because it contains the widest range of new and reused content blocks required across all four Upper Tier pages. This allows us to fully design, validate, and refine the core layout, visual system, and block interactions in a single, comprehensive mockup.

To ensure no content or functionality is overlooked, the Retailers page mockup will also include any additional, page-specific blocks that may not appear on that page but are required on the Manufacturers, Contractors, or Waste Facilities pages. These elements will be included as supplemental sections within the same Figma file so they can be reviewed and approved alongside the primary layout.

By finalizing all design decisions within one representative mockup, we can efficiently and consistently apply the approved design system to the remaining Upper Tier pages during development.

---

### Deliverables

4Site will deliver high-fidelity desktop and mobile mockups for one representative Upper Tier page, the Retailers page, including:

Desktop and mobile mockups for the Retailers page
Incorporation of consolidated client feedback
Internal alignment and clear handoff to development

While only one page will be reviewed client-facing during the design phase, the approved design system will be applied to the remaining Upper Tier pages during development.

All mockups will be produced in Figma. PaintCare will provide consolidated feedback directly within the Figma files.

---

### Design QA & Approval Process

4Site will create desktop and mobile mockups for the Retailers page, with up to two rounds of design revisions, following the process outlined below.

**Mockup Review**
4Site will present the desktop and mobile mockups for the Retailers page in Figma. Live feedback will be incorporated and shared with PaintCare for any additional review.

**Client Feedback**
PaintCare will provide consolidated feedback directly within the Figma files.

**Design Approval**
4Site will incorporate feedback into a revised, final design.
PaintCare will provide formal approval.

Approval of the Retailers page establishes the design standard for all remaining Upper Tier pages. Once the representative page is approved, no additional design revisions or feedback will be required prior to development. All approved design decisions will be applied consistently across the remaining Upper Tier pages during the development phase.
