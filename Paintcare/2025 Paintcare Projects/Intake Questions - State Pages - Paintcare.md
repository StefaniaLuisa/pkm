## Introduction

PaintCare is planning a redesign and restructure of its State Pages to improve usability, flexibility, and overall user experience. While we initially identified the “Additional Information” section as an opportunity for improvement, there’s broader interest in enhancing the State Pages as a whole. The current setup could benefit from improved navigation, streamlined resources, and a structure that reflects evolving state requirements, including updates expected in product acceptance for some states.

Potential updates include an in-page menu with standardized sections, a search or filter function, and restructured content to ensure information remains accessible on-page. The redesign will focus on creating a cohesive and adaptable experience that is easier to manage and aligns with PaintCare’s goals for consistency, clarity, and scalability.

## About this Project

1. Please give us a brief history of how the state pages came to be.

PaintCare operates recycling programs in states that have passed stewardship law. Each of these individual states has their own page.

In addition to the main stage pages, we also have state-specific Official Docs pages that are required by each state’s oversight agency.

2. Please list all stakeholders within this team and their roles, related to the state pages:

PaintCare has regional (e.g. Director of Western States) and staff-specific staff (e.g. California Program Manager) that are responsible for all things related to their states and will provide content updates for these pages, but the PaintCare Communications Team (Chelsea) updates and maintains this information on the website.
