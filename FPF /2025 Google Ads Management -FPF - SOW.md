# Project Description
**The Future of Privacy Forum (FPF) is looking for ongoing support managing their Google Ads, both paid and grant accounts, with a focus on implementing and optimizing advertising efforts to boost visibility and attendance for their Training Events Program.**
# Proposal
To make sure we’re aligned on expectations and results, 4Site Studios proposes an initial three-month period to assess the effectiveness of our Google Ads management for the Training Program. This trial period will allow us to evaluate our work together, with the goal of a successful partnership and the potential to extend our Google Ads work through 2025.
# Strategy
4Site’s goal is to support FPF’s Training Events Program through management and support of their Google Ads and Google Grants initiatives. 4Site will focus on increasing attendance, expanding audience reach, and optimizing ad spend to maximize the success of the campaigns within the $2,500 - $3,000 monthly ad spend budget.

# Deliverables
# Account Review & Setup
* Management of search campaigns across Google Ads.
* Audit and review the current structure of the ads, keywords and tracking
* Google Grants Setup & Management

#### ⠀Campaign Strategy & Implementation
* Focus FPF’s Google Paid account increasing registrations.  
* Use the Grants account to drive more traffic to the Training Event website section and pages. 
* Structure and create ad campaigns to align with FPF’s audience segments and desired event registrations goals.
* Perform keyword research aligned with FPF’s Training Events and FPF branding
* Add new keywords as relevant and exclude negative keywords to optimize ad reach.
* Ad Copywriting, 4Site will write and revise ad copy to maximize engagement and Click-Through Rate (CTR).
* Weekly monitoring to ensure campaigns are on track.
* Refine campaigns monthly through data insights, including adjusting bids, targeting, and placements for optimum performance
* Expand FPF’s audience reach through demographic and interest-based targeting.
* Explore and target new audience segments that align with FPF’s mission and training content.
* Provide recommendations on landing page design and functionality to improve ad performance and increase conversion rates for training sign-ups.

#### ⠀Reporting
* Monthly Performance Reports that provide details on ad performance, covering key metrics such as Click-Through Rate (CTR), Cost-Per-Click (CPC), conversions, and audience engagement.
* We will provide actionable insights to refine strategies and enhance campaign results.
* We will also highlight any potential adjustments needed for upcoming campaigns or Training promotions.

## ⠀Assumptions 
* FPF will provide access to Google ads and any relevant platforms, and participate actively in feedback sessions.
* FPF will provide timely feedback on ad copy and landing page recommendations to ensure efficient campaign optimization.
* A monthly ad spend budget of $2.5 - 3K has been allocated by FPF for paid advertising campaigns. 
* FPF is aware of Google Ads’[overdelivery feature](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/1704443?hl=en&sjid=1716653988752644353-NA#zippy=%2Cwill-overdelivery-of-my-average-daily-budget-increase-the-maximum-that-i-can-be-charged-for-the-month%2Cwhen-exactly-will-my-spend-exceed-my-average-daily-budget) where they may show ads more on days with high potential for conversions - causing an overspend. (~[Documentation](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/2375423?hl=en)~)
  * Google ensures that the user will never pay more than 2x your daily budget in any one day, or for Smart campaigns 4x your daily budget. The average for a calendar month will never be more than 30.4 your daily budget.
  * However, overspend can happen especially if not having enough dedicated time to reviewing account and ads. 
* All communication between stakeholders of this project will continue to take place in Productive.io
