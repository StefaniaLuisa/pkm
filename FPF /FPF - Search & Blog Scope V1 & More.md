
### Scope of Work: Blog Search and Categorization Optimization

**Client:** Future of Privacy Forum (FPF)
**Project:** Blog Search and Categorization Optimization
**Website:** https://fpf.org/blog/

---

### Project Overview

The goal of this project is to optimize and restructure the Future of Privacy Forum (FPF) blog to enhance user experience through improved search capabilities, refined categorization, and enhanced content discoverability.

---

### Objectives

- Enhance user engagement by improving blog search functionality.

- Expand and refine content categorization and tagging.

- Improve discoverability and SEO of older and underutilized content.

---

### Project Scope

#### 1. Enhanced Search Functionality

Utilize the existing WordPress plugin (SearchWP) to:

- Enable filtering by Content Type (Reports, Blogs, Events, Case Studies, Press Releases).

- Add Topic or Theme filtering (AI, Data Privacy, Regulation Updates, etc.).

- Implement Date Range filtering (Past Month, Past Year, Custom Range).

- Refine Author filtering, prioritizing current FPF contributors through integration with Co-Authors Plus and dynamic author management.

#### 2. Audit and Refine Blog Categorization

- Conduct an audit of current blog categories to remove redundancy and clarify structure.

- Standardize categories and clearly define subcategories.

- Develop and implement a consistent tagging strategy for enhanced content discoverability.

#### 3. SEO Content Optimization

- Identify and optimize legacy content with relevant keywords and updated metadata.

- Update existing blog posts to ensure accuracy and relevance.

- Implement structured data markup to enhance SEO visibility.

#### 4. Technical Implementation

- Configure and enhance existing SearchWP integration to support advanced filtering.

- Develop custom date-range filtering functionality via third-party tools (e.g., FacetWP).

- Integrate and configure Co-Authors Plus for improved author management and filtering.

- Ensure responsive and accessible design for all filtering and search functionalities.

---

### Deliverables

- Enhanced search interface with advanced filtering capabilities.

- Refined and standardized blog categorization system.

- Comprehensive SEO optimization of existing and legacy blog content.

- Technical documentation outlining the implemented customizations and configurations.

---

### Timeline

- Project Kickoff and Audit: 1 Week

- Search Functionality Enhancement: 2 Weeks

- Category and Tagging Optimization: 2 Weeks

- SEO Content Updates: Ongoing (initial optimization within 3 Weeks)

- Technical Integration and Testing: 2 Weeks

- Final Review and Deployment: 1 Week

Total Estimated Duration: **8-10 Weeks**

---

### Assumptions

- Client will provide necessary access to WordPress backend, plugins, and content.

- Timely feedback and approvals will be provided by the client throughout the project lifecycle.

- All content revisions and author updates will be reviewed and approved by FPF.

---

### Exclusions

- Creation of new blog posts or original content.

- Major design overhauls beyond necessary adjustments for filtering and responsiveness.

- Post-project support beyond agreed initial deployment and verification period.
