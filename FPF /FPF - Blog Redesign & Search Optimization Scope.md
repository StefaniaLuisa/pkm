*Prepared by Stef Jones, 4Site Interactive Studios, Inc.*

# Project Description

Future of Privacy Forum’s (FPF) would like to strategically improve and optimize their current blog, specifically looking to improve how users search for, navigate, and discover content. Over time, the blog has grown into a rich resource, but current limitations in search functionality and post organization have made it difficult for users to find relevant articles, especially older or thematically related posts.

Leveraging the advanced features that are already available through FPF’s SearchWP Plugin, 4Site’s work will focus on strategizing a more intentional taxonomy, improving internal search behavior, and rethinking how posts are categorized and tagged to ensure they reflect the breadth and depth of FPF’s work. The end result will be a more intuitive user experience that connects visitors with the information they’re looking for, with less friction and provide them with the insights they didn’t know they needed.

## Project Goals

- Improve search and filtering functionality to help users quickly find relevant posts, authors, and topics
  Establish a clear, scalable tagging and categorization system that reflects FPF’s key issues and research areas, while making it easier for staff to manage and optimize content

- Reorganize existing blog content to improve clarity, consistency, and overall user experience

- Assist with strategizing the use of older or underutilized posts to boost discoverability, engagement, and long-term SEO performance
