## Federal Resources

**U.S. Department of Labor — Wage & Hour Division** The primary federal enforcement agency for FLSA travel time and wage rules. <https://www.dol.gov/agencies/whd>

**FLSA Travel Time Fact Sheet (Fact Sheet #22)** The DOL's official guidance on what counts as compensable travel time under federal law. <https://www.dol.gov/agencies/whd/fact-sheets/22-flsa-hours-worked>

**FLSA Hours Worked Advisor** Interactive DOL tool that walks through what constitutes "hours worked."<https://webapps.dol.gov/elaws/whd/flsa/hoursworked/>

**Portal-to-Portal Act (29 U.S.C. §§ 251–262)** The federal statute that defines the limits of compensable commute vs. travel time. <https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?path=/prelim@title29/chapter8&edition=prelim>

**29 C.F.R. § 785.39 — Travel Away from Home** The specific federal regulation covering overnight/conference travel as compensable time. <https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-29/subtitle-B/chapter-V/subchapter-A/part-785/section-785.39>

**DOL Opinion Letter FLSA2020-19** Addresses compensable travel time for employees working at remote job sites — directly relevant. <https://www.dol.gov/agencies/whd/opinion-letters/flsa/2020/FLSA2020-19.pdf>

## 7th Circuit (Illinois-Specific Federal Ruling)

**Walters v. Professional Labor Group, LLC (7th Cir. 2024)** The most directly applicable recent ruling — the 7th Circuit (which covers Illinois) held that overnight conference/assignment travel during normal working hours must be compensated. <https://scholar.google.com/scholar_case?q=Walters+v.+Professional+Labor+Group+7th+circuit>

## Illinois State Resources

**Illinois Wage Payment and Collection Act (820 ILCS 115)** The primary Illinois statute requiring employers to pay all wages owed, including for hours worked. <https://www.ilga.gov/legislation/ilcs/ilcs3.asp?ActID=2402&ChapterID=68>

**Illinois Minimum Wage Law (820 ILCS 105)** Establishes Illinois standards for compensable time and minimum wage obligations. <https://www.ilga.gov/legislation/ilcs/ilcs3.asp?ActID=2121&ChapterID=68>

**Illinois Department of Labor — Official Site** The state agency that enforces wage and hour laws for Illinois workers.<https://labor.illinois.gov>

**Illinois Department of Labor — File a Wage Complaint** Direct link to file a formal complaint if the policy results in lost wages. <https://labor.illinois.gov/content/dam/soi/en/web/idol/docs/wage-claim-application.pdf>
